📖 How to Play
  • Each player starts with 7 tiles. Place a tile by matching one of its pip ends to an open end of the chain.
  • If you can't play, draw from the boneyard until you can — or pass if it's empty.
  • First player to empty their hand wins the round and scores the total pips left in opponents' hands.
  • If no one can play (blocked game), the player with the fewest pips wins.
  • First player to reach the target score wins the game!
