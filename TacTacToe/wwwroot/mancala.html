📖 How to Play

  • On your turn, click any of your pits (bottom row) that contain stones.
  • Stones are distributed counter-clockwise, one per pit.
  • Your store (right side) collects your points — opponent's store is skipped.
  • Extra turn: If your last stone lands in your store, play again!
  • Capture: Last stone in an empty pit on your side captures opposite stones.
  • Game ends when one side is empty. Most stones in store wins.
