TacTacToe

Rock Paper Scissors + Power-Ups

Single-player (vs AI) or local multiplayer on one device.

How to Play

  1. Pick match format, difficulty/mode, and power-up distribution, then start.
  2. Each round, choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors.
  3. You may activate one power-up per round (or none).
  4. Power-ups resolve in this fixed order: Sabotage → Lock-In → Reveal → Reverse → Randomizer → Shield → Double Strike → Charge.
  5. In timed mode, the match ends at 60 seconds. In best-of mode, first to required wins ends the match.
  6. In shop mode, wins give coins. Buy power-ups before each round (cost 1-3 coins).
