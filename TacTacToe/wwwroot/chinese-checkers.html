🎲 Chinese Checkers — Rules & Scoring

Objective

Be the first player to move all 10 of your marbles from your starting triangle (home corner) into the opposite triangle across the board.

How to Move

  • Step: Move one marble one space to any adjacent empty hole.
  • Jump: Leap over an adjacent marble (any colour) into the empty hole directly beyond it. You may chain multiple jumps in a single turn — all with the same marble.

Goal Zone Rules

  • Once a marble enters your destination triangle it cannot leave.
  • Marbles in the destination triangle may still hop within it to make room for your remaining marbles.

Winning

The first player to fill their destination triangle with all 10 marbles wins. Play continues to determine 2nd, 3rd place, etc.

Scoring

Your score reflects how far your marbles have advanced:

  • Each marble scores 8 − distance to its nearest destination hole (0–8 points per marble).
  • Filling your destination triangle completely earns a +100 bonus.
  • Maximum possible score: 10 × 8 + 100 = 180 points.

Tips

  • Look for long jump chains — they advance marbles without using extra turns.
  • Fill your destination triangle from the tip inward so you never block yourself.
  • Use the 💡 Hint button if you get stuck.
