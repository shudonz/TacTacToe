📖 How to Play

  • Click a column (or the arrow above it) to drop your disc.
  • Discs fall to the lowest available slot — like a real Connect 4 board!
  • First to connect 4 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins.
  • Use the 💡 Hint button if you're stuck — it highlights the best column.
  • The game ends in a draw if the board fills up with no winner.
